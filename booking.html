Advice

When booking hotels and Airbnbs it is advised that it is best to book those rooms way advice of your trip just in case others are looking at those same rooms and have the chance to book before you.

Group Travel Advice

  • House/Airbnb

    • 1. Airbnb often provides more space and a homely environment suitable for groups. 2. It allows group members to share common spaces, making it a more social experience. 3. Great for groups who prefer a more flexible and personalized stay.

  • Multiple Hotel Rooms

    • 1. Ideal for groups that prefer individual privacy and hotel amenities. 2. Easier to manage logistics and catering services for larger groups. 3. Some hotels offer interconnected rooms, providing a balance between privacy and group cohesion.

Pet-Friendly and Family Hotels

  1. Check hotel policies

    2. 1. Look for hotels that explicitly mention being pet-friendly. 2. Check if there are any size or breed restrictions. 3. Also check if the hotel has any amenities that are for children like pools, water slides, child room etc. Examples: COCO-MAT Athens BC, Perianth Hotel, Hotel Sunny Villas, Hotel Thira, Dream Island Hotel, and Creta Maris Resort.

  2. Research pet-friendly and family oriented areas

    3. 1. Some areas may have more pet-friendly facilities than others. Also, some places may not allow children to be inside such as restaurants, etc.

  3. Contact the hotel directly

    4. 1. Confirm their policies and inquire about any additional fees.
